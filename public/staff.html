About this portal

Use the dial on your dashboard to check in each morning and check out at the end of your shift. If you need to leave before 16:00, the system will ask for a brief reason — this is sent straight to the HR office for approval, and you can track its status under Early Checkouts at any time.

Your Monthly Report updates automatically as you check in and out, so you always know exactly where your attendance and punctuality stand.

Leaving early?

Your shift hasn't ended yet — let HR know why so they can review and approve it.
